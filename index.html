Our Works and Architecture

Los Angeles Aerial Rapid Transit

Los Angeles Aerial Rapid Transit (LA ART) is a proposal for an aerial rapid transit gondola system that will connect from Los Angeles Union Station and Dodger Stadium.

California High-Speed Rail

California High-Speed Rail will transform the way Californians travel the state with a clean, alternative form of transportation.

Dusit D2 Hua Hin Hotel

Dusit D2 hotel is located in Hua Hin, one of the popular provinces for family vacations in Thailand. It is a very nice hotel.

AEME Building / Paz Arquitectura

AEME is a building located in Guatemala City. It has an open floor plan on the first floor dedicated to commercial use and restaurants, a second floor that combines commerce and offices, and three floors exclusively dedicated to offices.

Victory Bay House

Victory Bay House nestled among ancient oaks on Kiawah's barrier island. It is a symbol of our architecture.

Matias House Intervention

An intervention that seeks to restore a single-family home located in a small-house neighborhood in San Miguel de Tucuman.
